Detailed Description

Bicubic spline fitter for data on a gridded polar disc.

Stores the grid coordinates , , the constant boundary radius , gridded function values , and the fitted B-spline representation. The origin value may optionally be prescribed (exactly or approximately), and zero-gradient boundary conditions at the origin can be enforced.
