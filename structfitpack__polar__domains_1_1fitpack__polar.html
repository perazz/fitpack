Detailed Description

Bicubic spline fitter for data scattered over a general polar domain.

Stores the scattered Cartesian data , the user-supplied boundary function , optional weights, and the fitted B-spline representation. The smoothing parameter controls the trade-off between closeness of fit and smoothness, while continuity at the origin ( ) is enforced up to order , , or .
