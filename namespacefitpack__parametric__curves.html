Detailed Description

OOP wrappers for parametric curve fitting in .

Provides three types for fitting parametric curves through data points in -dimensional space ( ):

  • fitpack_parametric_curve — open parametric spline (parcur)
  • fitpack_closed_curve — closed (periodic) parametric spline (clocur)
  • fitpack_constrained_curve — parametric spline with derivative constraints at endpoints (concur)

Each component of the curve is represented as a B-spline of degree over a common knot vector, with the parameter either user-supplied or automatically computed from cumulative chord lengths.

See also
Dierckx, Ch. 9 (pp. 199–228); parcur, clocur, concur
