Detailed Description

Bicubic spline fitter for data scattered on the sphere.

Stores the scattered spherical data , optional weights, and the fitted B-spline representation. The smoothing parameter controls closeness of fit versus smoothness. Pole constraints ensure regularity at (north pole) and (south pole).
